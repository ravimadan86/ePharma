Why do I need a Written Prescription?

If a product is licensed in the Canada, it can only be supplied against a doctor Prescription. Any products on the website that require a prescription will have the red stop sign next to the photo of them, and will indicate that the legal category is ‘POM-V’. We only accept prescriptions from doctors with a Canada/USA registration, where the owner’s address is within the Canada.

How long does a Written Prescription last for?

Unless your doctor specifies a shorter expiry, written prescriptions are only valid for 6 months from the date of issue. However, we can only supply up to the amount your doctors have prescribed. For example, if your prescription is only written for 30 tablets but is set to expire in 6 months, we can only legally dispense 30 tablets within these 6 months. Prescriptions for some scheduled drugs, such as Epiphen, are only valid for 28 days.
